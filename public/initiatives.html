Shiksha Sankalp

Education Community Ongoing

Empowering underprivileged children in Ahmedabad through quality education, digital literacy programs, and scholarship opportunities.

Our flagship education initiative focuses on bridging the educational gap in rural and urban slums around Ahmedabad. We provide free tutoring, distribute educational materials, and run computer literacy programs for children aged 6-16. The program has established learning centers in Maninagar, Naroda, and Vatva areas.

Key Activities: After-school tutoring, digital skills training, scholarship distribution, educational material supply, parent awareness sessions.

Jeewan Daan

Healthcare Blood Donation Life Saving

Organizing regular blood donation camps and maintaining a donor database to ensure timely blood availability for emergency cases in Ahmedabad hospitals.

Jeewan Daan operates as a bridge between blood donors and those in need. We organize monthly blood donation camps at colleges, offices, and community centers across Ahmedabad, maintaining a 24/7 helpline for emergency blood requirements.

Services: Regular blood donation camps, emergency blood donor coordination, blood type database management, donor health monitoring, awareness campaigns about voluntary blood donation.
